Who We Are

Counselor’s Corner, led by Dr. Wali Chidinma Beauty, provides professional guidance and counselling services to students and individuals seeking support in academics, careers, and personal growth.

About the Counselor

Dr. Wali Chidinma Beauty

Certified Educational Guidance Counselor

BSC/ M.Ed / Ph.D(Education)

Specializing in Academic, Career, and vocational Counseling

Our Major Counselling Areas

Academic Counseling

Guidance on course selection, study habits, time management, and goal-setting to support learning and achievement.

Career & Vocational Counseling

Helping individuals explore careers, plan for the future, and develop skills for practical, fulfilling paths.

Personal & Social Counseling

Support for emotional well-being, self-esteem, interpersonal skills, and personal challenges including family concerns or peer pressure.

Mission

To guide individuals in making informed educational, career, and vocational choices through accessible, professional counselling.

Vision

To become a trusted platform supporting holistic development in academic, vocational, and personal growth.
